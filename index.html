About the author

Meet D.T. Rocca

D.T. Rocca is a debut novelist — however, not at the start of a writing career — and D.T. is doing it differently.

D.T. grew up between playlists and paperbacks, and never quite decided which was loved more. So, D.T. stopped choosing. D.T.'s fiction blends sharp, contemporary storytelling with original music written to live inside the pages.

When D.T. isn't writing, D.T. is combing the beach looking for relics or shark's teeth, and listening to the calming sounds the ocean provides.
